Porsche

Porsche's brand heritage includes some of the most famous cars ever made. Porsche's relentless dedication to driver enjoyment above all else has enabled the company to produce cars that provide the ideal balance of supercar and daily driver.


My favorite out of Porsche's entire lineup is the 911. It is the exact middle of all cars Porsche has ever made- sitting evenly between their LeMans-winning racecars and their affordable SUVs. And yet, the car is far from mediocre. No car has done more for a brand than the 911 has for Porsche, and for good reason. Their timeless dedication to improvement without any compromises in driveability and performance have made the 911 an icon which has stood against the test of time.

The 911

The Evolution of Porsche's Timeless Design


Porsche has committed itself to continual improvement across the 911 lineup. This strategy of small improvement over time, without any daring leaps in style or performance, has made the 911 a steadfast icon of stability in a turbulent car world where outside influences typically rule the design and layout of cars.
