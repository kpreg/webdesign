What Is Torque? In the context of a car, torque is what provides the "oomph", or the power which shoves you in your seat during hard acceleration. Horsepower is a function of Torque. What is a good 0-60 time? Anything under 5 seconds is considered to be sporty, while anything under 3.5 seconds is considered a supercar. The fastest 0-60, which is currently held by the Dodge Challenger Hellcat, is 2.3 seconds.

What is a V12? Why is it important? A V12 Engine is an engine with 12 cylinders. Typically, V12 engines are found in high end exotic cars due to their complexity to manufacture and high cost of materials. Most are also in a twin turbo configuration, which takes advantage of the engine's large amount of waste exhuast gas and uses it to force more air into the engine. However, Ferrari and Lamborghini still use naturally apsirated (no turbochargers or superchargers) engines.
